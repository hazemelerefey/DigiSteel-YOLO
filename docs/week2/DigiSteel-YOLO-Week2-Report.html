Key Findings

Texture branch matters
Dual-branch DAFE (75.9%) beats edge-only (73.8%) by +2.1%. Texture features (local variance) help detect surface anomalies like pitting and scale.
DAFE matches but doesn't beat baseline
Dual-branch DAFE (75.9%) is essentially tied with baseline (75.8%). The added complexity (0.36M parameters) doesn't improve clean accuracy.
Edge-only hurts precision
Edge-only DAFE precision drops to 69.6% (vs 74.3% baseline). Sobel-initialized convolution alone is too restrictive for this dataset.
Why DAFE Doesn't Beat Baseline

Small dataset (1,800 images): Extra parameters may cause overfitting. Low contrast (28.9/255): Defects are hard to see regardless of architecture. Baseline already good: YOLOv11n with imgsz=800 extracts sufficient features.

5 Honest Evaluation: Training CSV vs Fresh Eval

The Problem

Training CSV records validation mAP during training, which can differ from standalone evaluation. We discovered significant inflation between the two methods.

Hypothesis vs Reality

Training CSV Numbers
Baseline v1: 75.6%
Baseline v2: 77.1%
DigiSteel DAFE: 76.0%
Appeared to show DAFE close to baseline
Fresh Evaluation (model.val())
Baseline v1: 71.3% (−4.4% gap)
Baseline v2: 75.8% (−1.2% gap)
DigiSteel DAFE: 75.9% (−0.1% gap)
True apples-to-apples comparison
Model Training CSV Fresh Eval Gap
Baseline v1 75.6% 71.3% −4.4%
Baseline v2 77.1% 75.8% −1.2%
DigiSteel DAFE 76.0% 75.9% −0.1%

Why the Difference?

Different evaluation settings
Training validation uses different settings than standalone model.val(), leading to inflated numbers.
Model degradation over epochs
The baseline model degraded from epoch 144 (77.1%) to epoch 291 (75.2%) during training.
Fresh eval is the gold standard
All results in this report use fresh evaluation (model.val()), providing the most accurate and reproducible comparison.
Lesson Learned

Training CSV numbers can be misleading. Fresh evaluation with consistent settings is critical for honest, reproducible comparisons. All results in this report use fresh evaluation only.
